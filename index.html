DRIVETRIBE


A digital hub for motoring. The unofficial DriveTribe app, an online community of tribes fueled by the passion for all forms of motoring that is led by the hosts of The Grand Tour. Find, post & discover articles, videos and interactions on motoring in every niche you can possibly imagine. Lead tribes, join some more and BE PART of it all. STAY DRIVEN.

Be Part

Drive Tribe is a "digital hub for motoring." It hosts video, articles, social media and interactive content, which will be organised around "tribes" with a unique tone of voice.

Who's came up with this?

DRIVETRIBE was co-founded by Clarkson, Hammond & May together with Andy Wilman, their long-time TV collaborator, and serial tech entrepreneur Ernesto Schmitt.

What is it again?

It is a brand new digital media platform consisting of a combination of content produced by celebrities of the motoring scene, professionally created content and that generated by users themselves.

“Gamers have got Twitch, travellers have got TripAdvisor and fashion fans have got, oh, something or other too. But people who are into cars have got nowhere. There’s no grand-scale online motoring community where people can meet and share video, comments, information and opinion. DriveTribe will change that. And then some.”

- Richard Hammond
